Frequently Asked Questions

Is ProImgResizer free to use?

Yes, all our basic tools are completely free to use. We may offer premium features in the future, but the core functionality will always remain free.

Are my images stored on your servers?

No. All image processing happens directly in your browser. Your images are never uploaded to our servers, ensuring complete privacy.

What image formats do you support?

We support all major image formats including JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP, and BMP. Our conversion tool can convert between these formats.

Is there a file size limit?

Since processing happens in your browser, the limit depends on your device's capabilities. Most modern devices can handle images up to 20-30MB without issues.
